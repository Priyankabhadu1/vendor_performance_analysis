# Vendor Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
The Vendor Performance Analysis Dashboard is a data analytics project designed to evaluate vendor performance, sales trends, inventory efficiency, and profitability using data visualization techniques.

The project uses Power BI to build an interactive dashboard that helps businesses monitor vendor contributions, identify low-performing brands, and optimize inventory and purchasing strategies.

This dashboard enables decision-makers to quickly understand key business metrics and take data-driven actions to improve operational efficiency and profitability.

---

## Business Problem

Effective inventory and sales management are critical for optimizing profitability in the retail and wholesale industry. Companies need to ensure that they are not incurring losses due to inefficient pricing, poor inventory turnover, or excessive dependency on certain vendors.

The goal of this analysis is to:

- Identify underperforming brands that require promotional or pricing adjustments.
- Determine top vendors contributing to sales and gross profit.
- Analyze the impact of bulk purchasing on unit costs.
- Assess inventory turnover to reduce holding costs and improve efficiency.
- Investigate the profitability variance between high-performing and low-performing vendors.

---

## Dashboard Overview

The Power BI dashboard provides a clear visualization of vendor and brand performance using several key performance indicators (KPIs).

### Key Metrics

- Total Sales: $441.41M  
- Total Purchase: $307.34M  
- Gross Profit: $134.07M  
- Profit Margin: 38.7%  
- Unsold Capital: $2.71M  

These KPIs help stakeholders quickly understand the overall financial performance of the business.

---

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Purchase Contribution %
A donut chart showing the percentage contribution of the top vendors to total purchases. This helps identify vendor dependency and major suppliers.

### 2. Top Vendors by Sales
A bar chart ranking vendors based on their total sales contribution, helping identify the most valuable vendors.

### 3. Top Brands by Sales
Displays the brands generating the highest revenue, helping businesses focus on high-performing products.

### 4. Low Performing Vendors
Highlights vendors whose performance scores are below acceptable thresholds, helping management take corrective action.

### 5. Low Performing Brands
A scatter plot used to analyze brand performance based on total sales and average profit margin.

---

## Key Insights

- A few vendors contribute significantly to total sales, indicating vendor concentration.
- Some brands generate high sales but lower profitability, suggesting pricing inefficiencies.
- Certain vendors consistently perform below expectations and may require contract review or replacement.
- Inventory optimization opportunities exist to reduce unsold capital and holding costs.
